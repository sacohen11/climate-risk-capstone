Can You Clarify?: Using NLP to Determine the Specificity of Climate-Related Financial Disclosures

By Sam Cohen, M.S.

Introduction

My capstone project was on identifying the specificity of sentences in Item 1A of SEC 10-K filings and comparing the specificity of sentences related to climate change risk vs. those not related to climate change risk. I looked at 260 companies over 7 years encompassing 1,420 10-K filings.
